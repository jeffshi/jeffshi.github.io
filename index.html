About Me

I am a military Operations Research Analyst separating from active duty on March 2017 and seeking to transition into a corporate Data Science career. I have civilian and military experience in software engineering, test and evaluation, geo-spatial analysis, project management, and Big Data analysis. I have a passion for problem solving, and a knack for interpreting useful information from complex data to senior leaders. On this page, you can find some projects I have completed as well as a link to my Resume and LinkedIn profile.

My work has driven the successful deployment of several Air Force acquisition programs, increased the effectiveness of military operations in South America, and supported operations in Iraq and Syria against the Islamic State (ISIS).

I hold an M.S in Industrial Engineering and a B.S in Computer Science and recently attained Six Sigma Black Belt certification. I am currently working on my M.S in Computer Science at Georgia Tech. I am experienced in SQL, R, Python, Java, VBA, and ArcGIS.
